AQIB ILYAS - PAKISTAN

HASHMATULLAH SHAHIDI

Aqib Ilyas Sulehri is a Pakistani-born cricketer who plays for the Oman national cricket team. He made his Twenty20 International debut for Oman against Hong Kong on 21 November 2015.

On 9 February 2020, in the 2020 Nepal Tri-Nation Series match against Nepal, Ilyas scored an unbeaten 109 runs from 108 balls. It was the first century by a batsman for Oman in ODI cricket. Two days later, against the United States, Ilyas scored his second century in as many matches, with 105 runs. In September 2021, he was named as the vice-captain of Oman's squad for the 2021 ICC Men's T20 World Cup.
